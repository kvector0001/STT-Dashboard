📊 1. ACCOUNTING RED FLAGS
- Revenue ↑ but Operating Cash Flow ↓
- Profit growth but weak cash flows
- Receivables growing faster than revenue
- Frequent accounting policy changes
- Large unexplained "Other Income"
- Inventory buildup / write-offs
- Off-balance-sheet liabilities

👥 2. GOVERNANCE RED FLAGS
- Promoter exits / stake reduction (>15–20%)
- OFS at deep discounts
- Promoter pledging (>30% risky)
- Related-party transactions (>10–15%)
- Loans to group entities
- Auditor resignation / frequent changes
- Qualified audit reports
- SEBI or regulatory action
- Weak board independence
- Past fraud history

💰 3. FINANCIAL RED FLAGS
- Debt rising faster than EBITDA
- Debt/EBITDA > 3–4x
- Interest coverage < 2x
- Persistent negative FCF
- Working capital stress
- Equity dilution for survival

🏭 4. BUSINESS RED FLAGS
- High customer concentration
- Weak or no moat
- Tech disruption risk
- Aggressive expansion without profits
- Heavy dependence on regulation or 1 segment
