## 📈 1. TREND STRENGTH
Evaluate:
- Price vs 50 DMA and 200 DMA
- Slope of moving averages
- Higher Highs / Higher Lows (uptrend) vs Lower Highs / Lower Lows (downtrend)

✅ Bullish: price above 50 & 200 DMA; 50 DMA above 200 DMA (golden structure); clean uptrend.
🚨 Bearish: price below key averages; 50 DMA below 200 DMA (death cross); lower highs / breakdown trend.

## 🚀 2. BREAKOUT / BREAKDOWN STRUCTURE
Check for BOTH directions:

Breakout (bullish):
- Consolidation / range breakout
- Base formation (flat base, cup & handle)
- Resistance / multi-month breakout
- ✅ Strong: long consolidation → breakout with tight price action
- 🚨 Weak: failed / fake breakout, no follow-through

Breakdown (bearish):
- Breakdown below a key support / range floor
- Topping patterns (head & shoulders, double top, rising-wedge break)
- Loss of 200 DMA / trendline support
- 🚨 Strong breakdown: clean support break with follow-through selling → sharp-fall risk
- ⚠️ Note any support that has just been lost or is about to be tested

## 📊 3. VOLUME CONFIRMATION
- Volume expansion on the breakout OR breakdown
- Volume vs average; accumulation vs distribution
- ✅ Strong move: breakout/breakdown supported by high volume
- 🚨 Distribution: heavy down-volume, selling-pressure spikes

## ⚡ 4. MOMENTUM
- RSI (40–60 neutral; >60 bullish strength; <40 weak; <30 oversold/breakdown momentum)
- MACD trend (if observable)
- Momentum divergence (bearish divergence = price up but momentum down)

## 🛡️ 5. RISK STRUCTURE
- Distance from / strength of support levels
- Is support holding or breaking?
- Stop-loss clarity and risk-reward
- ✅ Good (long): nearby strong support, controlled downside
- 🚨 Risky: extended rally far from support, OR support already broken with open downside
