## 👤 1. PROMOTER PROFILE & TENURE
Evaluate:
- Promoter background, experience
- Years in business / industry
- Reputation (if verifiable)
- Leadership stability

✅ Positive:
- Long, consistent leadership
- Clean track record

🚨 Red Flags:
- Frequent management changes
- Limited disclosure
- Past controversies

## 📉 2. OWNERSHIP BEHAVIOUR (MOST IMPORTANT SIGNAL)
Analyse:
- Promoter holding trend (increasing / decreasing)
- Bulk deals / OFS / stake sales
- Sudden large exits (>10–20%)
- Promoter pledging

✅ Positive:
- Increasing or stable stake
- Low/no pledging

🚨 Strong Red Flags:
- Continuous promoter selling
- Large stake dilution
- OFS at discount
- Pledging >30%
- Selling during strong business narrative

⚠️ This section should heavily influence the final score.

## 💸 3. CAPITAL ALLOCATION DISCIPLINE
Evaluate:
- Reinvestment quality (ROCE on incremental capital)
- Dividends / buybacks vs reckless expansion
- Acquisitions — value-accretive or empire-building?
- Debt usage and balance-sheet discipline

## 🏛️ 4. GOVERNANCE & TRANSPARENCY
Evaluate:
- Related-party transactions
- Board independence
- Auditor quality / changes / qualifications
- Disclosure quality and consistency
- Regulatory / SEBI history

## 🎯 5. GUIDANCE vs EXECUTION
Evaluate:
- Track record of meeting guidance
- Consistency of commentary vs delivery
- Honesty in acknowledging misses
